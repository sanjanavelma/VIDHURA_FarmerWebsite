**🌱 VIDHURA – Farmer Support & Sustainable Farming Platform**
VIDHURA is a web-based platform designed to empower farmers with modern technology and sustainable farming practices.  
The platform connects **farmers, consumers, and agricultural students** while providing tools such as **AI-based crop disease detection, marketplace access, and educational resources**.
Our mission is to make farming more **efficient, profitable, and sustainable** by combining technology with agricultural knowledge.

🚜 Features
 1. AI-Based Crop Disease Detection
Farmers can upload images of crops to detect plant diseases using AI models and receive treatment suggestions.

2. Farmer-to-Consumer Marketplace
Farmers can directly sell their produce to consumers without middlemen, ensuring better prices.

3. Second-Hand Equipment Exchange
A platform where farmers can buy and sell used agricultural equipment at affordable prices.

4. Educational Resources
Blogs and articles provide knowledge about sustainable farming practices and crop management.

5. AI Chatbot Support
A chatbot assists farmers with queries related to crop health, farming techniques, and agricultural guidance.

6. Community Engagement
Farmers, students, and experts can collaborate and share knowledge.
